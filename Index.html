Their (Slightly Ridiculous) Story
tap each frame to read the unfortunate truth
1
FIRST MEETING
A "meet-and-greet" lunch at a hotel both our families swore was "fancy." Just the two of us at the table. The biryani was so dry the rice gave up halfway. The rasam was a war crime. We bonded over a shared, silent eyebrow-raise across the table — and that, somehow, was enough. We still laugh about it. The hotel does not.
2
SECOND MEETING
Was technically our engagement ceremony. Most couples have this thing called "dating" — we had matching outfits and 200 watching relatives. We were stared at for three hours straight by people whose names we did not know. Said two sentences to each other. One of them was, "your shoes are nice." We meant it.
3
AND NOW
Most of our "dating" is video calls where one of us is muted on accident for 14 minutes. Our mothers WhatsApp each other more than we do. We've discovered he likes the AC at 29°C and she likes it at -10°C. There is no compromise. There will be war. Honestly? We're figuring it out. Slowly. With great snacks.
